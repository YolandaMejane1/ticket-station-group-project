Easy Tickets was Founded in 2024 by four young and ambitious developers who shared a passion for innovation and simplifying event ticketing. With a combined expertise in software development,design,and entrepreneurship, Our team is set out to revolutionize the way people purchase and manage event tickets.

Our Story

Our journey began with a simple idea: to make event ticketing easier and more accessible to everyone. As avid event-goers ourselves, we were frustrated with the complexities and inefficiencies of traditional ticketing platforms. We saw an opportunity to create a seamless, user-friendly experience that would connect event organizers and attendees like never before.

With limited resources and unwavering determination, they pooled their knowledge and skills to create a platform that would address the challenges they faced as event-goers. They recognized the need for a user-friendly, affordable, and accessible ticketing system that would connect event organizers with potential attendees.

Payment Information

What payment methods do you accept?

At EasyTickets, we strive to provide a seamless and convenient payment experience for our customers. We accept major credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

Additionally, we offer secure online payment options through trusted payment gateways like PayPal and Ozow.

To ensure transparency, we clearly display all applicable fees during the checkout process. Easy Tickets charges a nominal service fee to cover the costs associated with maintaining our secure ticketing platform and providing exceptional customer service.
